Getting Started

Step 1: Setup

  1. Connect MetaMask to Sepolia network
  2. Get free test tokens from our faucet
  3. Ensure you have some Sepolia ETH for gas

Step 2: Start Trading

  1. Select your input and output tokens
  2. Enter the amount you want to trade
  3. Review the route and price impact
  4. Execute your first swap!
